For example, suppose you toss a fair coin: Heads, you win 25 cents, Tails, you lose 25 cents. The probability of getting Heads is \(1/2\text{,}\) as is the probability of getting Tails. The expected value of the game is

\begin{equation*} \biggl(\frac{1}{2}\times .25\biggr)+\biggl(\frac{1}{2}\times(- .25)\biggr)=0. \end{equation*}
